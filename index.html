The Threat Is Real

On March 14, 2025, the White House issued an executive order to "eliminate" the Institute of Museum and Library Services (IMLS), which provides critical funding to libraries nationwide. Section 2(a)(iv) of the order specifically targets IMLS for reduction "to the minimum presence and function required by law."

Without IMLS funding, libraries across the country face devastating budget cuts, reduced services, and possible closures. This is not a hypothetical threatâ€”it's happening now, and we need your help to stop it.
