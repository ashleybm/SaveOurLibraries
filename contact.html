Frequently Asked Questions

How can I order postcards?

You can find all the information about our postcard program, including how to order, on our Postcard Program page.

Can I contribute content to the website?

Yes! We welcome contributions of articles, resources, and success stories. Please use the contact form above to share your ideas.

How can I get involved beyond what's on the website?

We're always looking for volunteers to help with content creation, outreach, and other aspects of our initiative. Please reach out using the contact form to discuss how you can contribute.

Is Save Our Libraries affiliated with any organizations?

Save Our Libraries is an independent grassroots initiative. We're not formally affiliated with any organizations, though we support the work of library advocacy groups across the country.

Can I use the resources on this website?

Yes! All content on this website is available for public use. We encourage you to use our resources for library advocacy. Attribution is appreciated but not required.
