About Our Sources

This bibliography includes official documents, research, reports, and other resources that inform our understanding of the current threats to libraries and the importance of library advocacy. We believe in evidence-based advocacy and transparency about our sources.

This list will be updated regularly as we gather more information. If you have suggestions for additional resources to include, please contact us.
