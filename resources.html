Resources

Tools and information to help you advocate for libraries

Phone Call Scripts

Use these scripts when calling your representatives to advocate for library funding:

Script for Local Representatives

"Hello, my name is [Your Name] and I'm a constituent from [Your City/Town]. I'm calling to express my support for our local libraries and to urge [Representative's Name] to prioritize library funding in the upcoming budget.

Libraries are essential community resources that provide access to information, education, and opportunity for all residents. Our local library [mention a specific service or program that's important to you] has been invaluable to me and many others in our community.

I strongly encourage [Representative's Name] to support increased funding for libraries to ensure they can continue to serve our community effectively. Thank you for your time and consideration."

Script for State Representatives

"Hello, my name is [Your Name] and I'm a constituent from [Your City/Town]. I'm calling to express my concern about library funding at the state level and to urge [Representative's Name] to support increased investment in our public libraries.

Libraries are vital educational and community resources that serve people of all ages and backgrounds. They provide essential services like internet access, job search assistance, and early literacy programs that benefit our entire state.

I strongly encourage [Representative's Name] to support legislation that increases funding for libraries and protects them from budget cuts. Thank you for your time and consideration."

Tips: Be polite, concise, and personal. Share a brief story about why libraries matter to you if you have time.

Social Media Scripts

Use these video script templates to create compelling social media content:

Personal Library Story (30 seconds)

"Hi, I'm [Your Name] and libraries have been a part of my life since [childhood memory or first experience]. My local library [specific example of how you use the library] and it's made a huge difference in my life.

Now, many libraries are facing budget cuts that threaten these essential services. If you value libraries like I do, please [specific call to action - contact representatives, attend a meeting, etc.].

Our libraries need us. Learn more at [website]. #SaveOurLibraries"

Library Facts Video (60 seconds)

"Did you know that public libraries are one of the most cost-effective public services we have? For just [local library cost per resident], our libraries provide:

- Free access to books, movies, and music
- Internet access and computer use
- Job search assistance and resume help
- Children's programs and educational support
- Community meeting spaces
- And so much more

Despite this incredible value, many libraries face budget cuts that threaten these services. If you believe in the power of libraries, please [specific call to action].

Learn more about how you can help at https://ashleybm.github.io/SaveOurLibraries/. #SaveOurLibraries2025"

Tips: Keep videos short, authentic, and include a clear call to action. Film in good lighting and use captions for accessibility.

Postcard Writing Tips

Effective postcard advocacy can make a real difference. Here's how to write impactful postcards:

Content Tips

  • Be personal: Share why libraries matter to you specifically
  • Be specific: Mention a particular program or service that's important
  • Be clear: State exactly what action you want the recipient to take
  • Be respectful: Maintain a polite and constructive tone
  • Be concise: Space is limited, so focus on your main message

Format Tips

  • Write legibly: Print clearly if your handwriting is difficult to read
  • Include your return address: This shows you're a constituent
  • Use the correct address: Double-check the recipient's office address
  • Sign your name: Add a personal touch with your signature
  • Add a hashtag: Include #SaveOurLibraries2025 to connect with the movement

Sample Postcard Text

Dear [Representative's Name],

Our local library helped me find my first job through their resume workshop. Libraries like ours provide essential services that benefit everyone in our community.

Please support increased funding for libraries in the upcoming budget. Our community depends on these vital resources.

Thank you,
[Your Name]
#SaveOurLibraries2025
