About Save Our Libraries

Our Mission

Save Our Libraries is a grassroots initiative created in direct response to the White House executive order "CONTINUING THE REDUCTION OF THE FEDERAL BUREAUCRACY" (March 14, 2025), which explicitly targets the Institute of Museum and Library Services (IMLS) for elimination. Our mission is to protect and preserve public libraries across the country from this immediate threat.

We aim to mobilize community members and voters to take urgent action in opposition to this executive order, specifically Section 2(a)(iv) which targets IMLS. We believe that libraries are essential community resources that must be defended from political attacks that threaten their existence.

The Current Threat

On March 14, 2025, the White House issued an executive order that specifically targets the Institute of Museum and Library Services for elimination. The order states that "the non-statutory components and functions of [IMLS] shall be eliminated" and statutory functions reduced "to the minimum presence and function required by law."

This is not a vague policy changeâ€”it's a direct attack on the primary federal funding source for libraries nationwide. Without IMLS:

  • Rural libraries will lose critical funding that keeps their doors open
  • Technology access in underserved communities will be severely limited
  • Educational programs for children and adults will be cut
  • Job search assistance and workforce development will be reduced
  • Community services that many vulnerable populations rely on will disappear

This executive order represents an existential threat to libraries across America, particularly in communities that need them most.

What We Do

Save Our Libraries provides resources and tools to help people advocate for their local libraries. We:

  • Curate and share information about library funding and policies
  • Create scripts for phone calls to representatives
  • Develop video scripts for social media advocacy
  • Provide tips for effective postcard writing campaigns
  • Distribute postcard packs at cost to support advocacy efforts

All of our resources are designed to be accessible and easy to use, even for those who have never engaged in advocacy before.

How You Can Get Involved

There are many ways to support Save Our Libraries and advocate for your local library:

  • Use our resources to contact your representatives
  • Share information about library advocacy on social media
  • Participate in our postcard campaign
  • Attend local library board meetings
  • Vote in local elections that impact library funding

Every action, no matter how small, helps to ensure that libraries remain vibrant and accessible for generations to come.
